Services

Welcome to Mellow Immigration Services, your trusted partner for immigration solutions.

Immigrate

Embark on a new journey with Mellow Immigration Services. Our team of experts ensures a smooth transition, helping you navigate the complexities of immigration laws and procedures.

Work

Unlock global opportunities with Mellow Immigration Services. We assist individuals in securing work permits and visas, enabling them to pursue their career aspirations abroad. Our dedicated team provides personalized guidance, ensuring you meet all requirements for a successful application.

Study

Achieve your academic goals with Mellow Immigration Services. We specialize in student visa applications, providing end-to-end support for individuals seeking to study abroad. Our knowledgeable team is committed to helping you secure admission in your dream institution and ensuring a seamless visa application process.

About

We are a licensed immigration service provider located in the Greater Toronto Area, ON, Canada.

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Our inception was driven by a commitment to navigate the complexities of Canadian immigration procedures with integrity, responsibility, and professionalism. We offer a comprehensive range of immigration services to individuals aspiring to call Canada their new home.

  • March 2011

    An Agency is Born

    Our mission is simple: to transform your Canadian dream into reality and facilitate your seamless integration into what we believe is the best country in the world.

  • December 2015

    Transition to Full Service

    Whether your goal is to work, study, or embark on a new business venture in Canada, we’re here to make that journey smooth and hassle-free.

  • July 2020

    Phase Two Expansion

    With our extensive knowledge and expertise in immigration processes, you can rely on us to guide you every step of the way.
